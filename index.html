Common Issues

App Not Loading

  • Force close and restart Moonly
  • Check for app updates in App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure iOS 15.0 or later

Dream Capture Issues

  • Pull down to refresh your dreams
  • Verify you're logged into the correct account
  • Check microphone permissions for voice recording
  • Force close and reopen if data isn't saving

Voice Recording Not Working

  • Enable microphone access in Settings → Moonly → Microphone
  • Check if your device has sufficient storage
  • Restart the app if recording fails
  • Try text entry as an alternative

Subscription & Premium

  • Managed through App Store subscriptions
  • Restore purchases in app settings
  • Contact Apple Support for billing issues
  • Email us for account problems

Privacy Policy for Moonly

Last updated: October 29, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Moonly, we believe your dreams belong to you. We've built our app with privacy at its core, ensuring your personal information remains private and secure while helping you capture and understand your dreams effectively.

What We DON'T Do

  • We DO NOT collect, store, or access your dream content on any servers
  • We DO NOT upload your dreams to the cloud without your explicit consent
  • We DO NOT share or sell your data with third parties
  • We DO NOT track your personal dream content remotely

How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only

All your personal dream information (dream entries, notes, tags, emotions, and journal entries) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your phone by default
  • Only you can access your personal information through the app
  • If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed
  • You control whether to enable optional encrypted cloud backup

Authentication Data

We only collect minimal information required for app functionality:

  • Email address (for authentication only)
  • Subscription status (to enable premium features)
  • Basic usage analytics (app crashes, performance metrics)

This data is securely managed through Firebase Authentication and is never linked to your personal dream content.

AI-Generated Shareable Cards

When you choose to generate shareable cards:

  • Only anonymized pattern data is processed (themes, emotions, frequencies)
  • Your actual dream content is never sent to AI services
  • Cards are generated based on aggregated statistics only
  • You can opt out of this feature at any time

Your Control and Responsibility

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You may choose to share information with others at your discretion
  • We are not responsible for data you choose to share outside the app
  • You can request account deletion by contacting help.sanai@gmail.com
  • You can immediately delete all your data by uninstalling the app

Data Deletion

You can delete your data in two ways:

Immediate deletion: Uninstall the app from your device

Account deletion: Submit a request to help.sanai@gmail.com with subject line "Moonly Account Deletion"

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Remove your authentication data from Firebase
  • Cancel any active subscriptions
  • Delete any associated account information

Third-Party Services

We use the following services with minimal data sharing:

  • Firebase Authentication (for login and account management)
  • Payment processors (RevenueCat, Apple App Store for subscriptions)
  • Firebase Analytics (for app performance monitoring)

These services have their own privacy policies and handle only the minimum data required for their functions.

Dream Content Disclaimer

Moonly is designed to help you capture and reflect on your dreams. We:

  • Do not provide psychological analysis or therapy
  • Do not replace professional mental health services
  • Recommend consulting healthcare professionals for psychological concerns
  • Are not responsible for interpretations or decisions based on dream content

International Users and Compliance

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

Since we don't store personal dream data on servers, most of these rights are automatically fulfilled by our local-only storage approach.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Security

While we don't store your personal dream content on our servers, we implement security measures for the data we do collect:

  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Secure payment processing through official app stores
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Optional encrypted cloud backup for your dream data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Posting notifications within the app
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes
